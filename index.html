New city, no scene

You travel — and the floor disappears.

You land in a new city ready to dance and hit a wall. Which nights are good? Where did the Tuesday social move to? Locals just know. Visitors get a cold map and a guess.

Locked behind the feed

No Instagram? You don't exist to the scene.

Half the events live only in an Instagram story or a private Facebook group. Not on social, or not following the right promoter? The night happens without you ever hearing about it.

So we built one honest map of the social-dance world — no Instagram, no Facebook, just a new way to find dancing.

Our story
“I've been social dancing for 20 years, and I built the app I always wished I had.”

For nearly 20 years, social dancing has been part of my life. Salsa, bachata, the whole world that opens up the first time you step onto a crowded dance floor at 11pm on a Tuesday and realize this scene exists in every city, if you can find it.

That last part has always been the problem.

Every time I traveled — a new city for work, a weekend trip, even abroad — the first thing I'd do is try to find where to dance that night. And every time, it was the same scavenger hunt: digging through Facebook groups that hadn't been updated in months, scrolling Instagram for flyers buried under last week's posts, asking strangers, hoping someone in a forum had posted something recent. Sometimes I'd find an incredible night. Sometimes I'd show up to an empty venue. Often I'd give up and miss out entirely.

It shouldn't be this hard to find a dance floor.

So I built Bailamos! — a single place to find local socials, classes, and events, whether you're home or visiting somewhere new. Built for the way dancers actually look for events: fast, local, and current.

I've been social dancing for 20 years, and I built the app I always wished I had.

— Rudy, Founder. Dancer for 20 years, software engineer for a living.
